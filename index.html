About

We are the Asymptones. We take a low-key approach to a cappella singing! The group was started in 2007 when a few MIT students started singing together for fun, and grew from there into our group today. The Asymptones are dedicated to being a low-time-commitment group that sings for fun! The group was started to let us sing fun songs without dedicating 6+ hours to rehearsal every week. Right now we sing only 3 hours per week! We sing everything from Guitar Hero hits to Disney songs to video game music and are constantly arranging new music in arranging parties.

Audition

Interested in auditioning? Fall auditions are held the weekend before term starts (come see us at the Fall Midway for details!) and we sometimes holds IAP or spring auditions depending on the year. Each audition is around 7 minutes long and involves some vocal warmups and excercises followed by a solo of your choice (it doesn't have to be nerdy)! Email us at asymptones-officers@mit.edu to schedule an audition or if you have any questions!
